Ingredients:
- 4 tbsp. of flour
- 3 tbsp. of sugar
- 2 tbsp. of cocoa powder
- 1/2 tsp. of baking powder
- tiny pinch of salt (to bring out the flavor)
- 3 tbsp. of milk
- 1 tbsp. of oil
- 1 tsp. of vanilla extract
- 1 tbsp. of hazelnut spread

Instructions:
1. combine all wet ingredients in a medium to big sized mug (except the hazelnut
spread)
2. combine all the dry ingredients (except hazelnut spread)
3. stir until well blended
4. add hazelnut spread into the middle of the mixture
5. place in microwave; cook for 2 minutes, or until desired texture
